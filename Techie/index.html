About Us

Safe IT is a junior company, founded by a first year science & technology student at the M'hamed Bougara Unversity of Boumerdes (UMBB), in order to participate in PHIKRA national competition in its first edition 2020. The company was created during the winter season. As a result, the team decided to make it an IT company that protects and saves lives from gas leaks from heating appliances. We have entered the Algerian market with a new approach to protection through our distinguished product "GASECURE" responds to the urgent need for safety.
